About Roy

Founded in 1901 and named after Frank Roy, the community's first postmaster, Roy grew with the arrival of the Dawson Railway and became an important center for ranching and commerce.


Today, Roy remains proud of its western heritage and continues to serve residents throughout Harding County.

Resident Services & Governance

🚰 Online Utility Payments

Secure credit and debit card payment routes for residential water, sewer, and trash balance billing are currently undergoing framework review.

Please continue to use the physical drop-box or standard mail handling until this gateway is activated by the board.

👕 Official Village Gift Shop

A localized print-on-demand community storefront featuring historic town apparel, custom high school sports items, and regional legacy memorabilia.

This system passes 100% of profit margins back into the official town treasury routing layout without requiring inventory space.

🔒 Municipal Staff Portal

Authenticated administrative access gateway restricted to the Village Clerk, Deputy Clerk, and department heads.

Enables direct, code-free uploading of town council meeting records, public PDF archives, and emergency safety notices.
