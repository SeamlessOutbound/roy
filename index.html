About Roy

Roy was founded in 1901 and named after Frank Roy, the community's first postmaster. The village grew following the arrival of the Dawson Railway and became an important center for ranching and commerce in northeastern New Mexico.


Today Roy remains proud of its heritage while serving residents across Harding County.


  • Population: Approximately 193
  • County: Harding County
  • Elevation: 5,896 ft
  • ZIP Code: 87743
