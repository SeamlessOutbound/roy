Resident Services & Governance

🚰 Online Utility Payments

The Village of Roy is preparing secure online payment processing for residential water, sewer, and trash services. Residents will be able to securely remit balances via credit/debit card directly to the municipal treasury.

Notice to Residents: Online transaction options are currently undergoing structural system integration. Please continue to utilize the Village Hall drop-box or mail services until the secure processor portal is declared live.
